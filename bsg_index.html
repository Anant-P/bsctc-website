Bharat Scouts and Guides, Uttar Pradesh, in collaboration with National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) , and NxtWave has launched a modern computer lab at Prayagraj. We're introducing industry-oriented courses like Animation and Web Development, for empowering young individuals with employable skills. This initiative aims to enhance future employment prospects and foster entrepreneurship, enabling students to develop their own opportunities and secure jobs.
